Short Term Loan:
Key Points
(Move your mouse over the cards)
It is important that STLs given for Working Capital purposes should be backed by sufficient drawing power.
Since the loans are short term in nature, the rates charged are comparatively lower and fixed (as time line increases uncertainty grows resulting into higher risk and so the return and vice versa).
These loans are generally repaid as bullet repayment at the end of the tenor with interest servicing on a monthly basis.
STL can be revolving or non-revolving depending upon the purpose. Usually, the loan is revolving for Working Capital requirements.
