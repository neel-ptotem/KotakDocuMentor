So, what is it?

Short Term Loan


  • As the name suggests, these loans are for short tenor, typically range from 30 days to 1 year.
  • These loans are availed by businesses to meet their short term requirement of funds.
  • These can be in form of cash flow mismatches (like some payment to be received is delayed), working capital, seasonal requirements, payments of dividends, salaries and other payments.
